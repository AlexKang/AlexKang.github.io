NookPhone Launcher

About

NookPhone Launcher is a fan-made app developed by Alex Kang and Angela Alberto. All assets are based off of or inspired by Nintendo's Animal Crossing: New Horizons. This app is not affiliated with any Nintendo properties.

Open Source

  • DragDropSwipeRecyclerView
  • Maskable Layout
  • Nunito (font)
  • RxJava

Privacy Policy

NookPhone Launcher collects no user data.
